# Academiya

Academiya is a modern academy management app designed for non-formal education centers, training institutes, and community-based learning organizations. It helps academies manage classes, students, subjects, schedules, instructors, and learning programs in one simple platform.

Inspired by the Filipino word for "academy," Academiya supports institutions that provide practical, skills-based, and professional education outside the traditional school system. Its goal is to make academy operations easier, more organized, and more accessible for both administrators and learners.
